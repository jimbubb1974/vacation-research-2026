## Weather / Crowds

July is peak summer in Bavaria and Austria. Expect warm to hot days, afternoon thunderstorms possible in the mountains, and heavy crowds at signature sites — especially Neuschwanstein.

Practical rules:
- Book Neuschwanstein timed entry immediately upon confirming the trip — July interior tours sell out weeks ahead
- Eagle's Nest operates May–October; check kehlsteinhaus.de for seasonal opening
- Hallstatt is extremely popular in July; arrive early or late in the day to beat tour buses

# Transportation Notes

- Book IAD ⇄ MUC round trip first (Lufthansa nonstop ~8h 10m; also check United)
- Bayern-Ticket covers Munich local trains and Neuschwanstein regional train (~€49/family/day; 14yo rides free; 17yo = adult)
- Neuschwanstein: train to Füssen + local bus; or direct Munich day-tour bus (easier logistics)
- Munich → Berchtesgaden: regional rail via Freilassing (~2h 30m; covered by Bayern-Ticket)
- Berchtesgaden → Salzburg: Bus 840 (~50 min; ~€11/pp; not covered by Bayern-Ticket)
- Salzburg → Munich departure: ÖBB Railjet (~1h 28m); book seat reservation 3–4 weeks ahead
- No rental car needed; all connections by rail and regional bus
