Daily Detail

Day 0 — Sat Jul 11 · Travel

  • 5:30 PM — Depart IAD, nonstop overnight flight
  • Arrive Munich 7:45 AM (+1)

Day 1 — Sun Jul 12 · Arrive Munich

  • 7:45 AM — Land MUC; train to Neuhausen-Nymphenburg apartment (~55 min)
  • Morning — Drop bags (official check-in not until 3 PM; coordinate with host)
  • ~11 AM — Marienplatz: Neues Rathaus Glockenspiel (chimes at 11 AM and noon)
  • Lunch — Viktualienmarkt open-air food market
  • Afternoon — Frauenkirche cathedral; wander the Altstadt
  • Evening — Light dinner; early night to recover from the overnight flight

Day 2 — Mon Jul 13 · Munich

  • Morning — Free; sleep in or wander
  • 11:30 AM — Classic Bike Tour: ~4 hours through English Garden, Olympiapark, and city highlights with a local guide. Helmets provided.
  • ~3:30 PM — Tour returns
  • Evening — Dinner at Hopferhouse (traditional Bavarian)

Day 3 — Tue Jul 14 · Munich → Königssee

  • 9:00–10:00 AM — Nymphenburg Palace state rooms (baroque royal residence)
  • 10:00–10:35 AM — Marstallmuseum: royal carriages + Nymphenburg Porcelain collection
  • 10:35–11:30 AM — Walk to Amalienburg (~15 min through the park); rococo hunting lodge — the silver Hall of Mirrors is the highlight of the estate
  • 11:30 AM–12:30 PM — Remaining park castles if energy allows (Badenburg, Pagodenburg) or a slow walk back along the canal
  • 12:30–1:15 PM — Lunch at Palmenhaus café (on-site in the Orangery)
  • Afternoon — Train to Berchtesgaden area (~3 hrs); bus to Schönau am Königssee; check in

Day 4 — Wed Jul 15 · Eagle's Nest + Salt Mine

  • Morning — Bus from Berchtesgaden to Obersalzberg; Kehlstein bus to Eagle's Nest summit (1,834m); panoramic Alpine views — allow 3–4 hrs total including return
  • Afternoon — Berchtesgaden salt mine (~1.5 hrs): mine carts, underground salt lake, wooden slide

Day 5 — Thu Jul 16 · Königssee

  • ~9–10 AM — Electric boat from Königssee dock to Salet (~50 min); passes the red-roofed St. Bartholomä chapel
  • Mid-morning — Walk from Salet to Obersee (~20 min, turquoise glacier lake)
  • Late morning — Continue to Rotbachfall (~30 min further): Germany's highest waterfall at ~470m
  • Midday — Return hike + boat back
  • Afternoon — Free in Schönau

Day 6 — Fri Jul 17 · → Salzburg

  • Morning — Checkout; taxi to Salzburg (~30 min)
  • Midday — Arrive Salzburg; check in
  • Afternoon — Altstadt (UNESCO World Heritage): Getreidegasse (Mozart's birthplace at No. 9, wrought-iron guild signs), Dom Cathedral, Residenzplatz
  • Evening — Easy dinner; settle in

Day 7 — Sat Jul 18 · Salzburg

  • Morning — Funicular to Fortress Hohensalzburg: medieval castle with sweeping views over city and Alps (~2 hrs)
  • Midday — Getreidegasse walk; lunch
  • Afternoon — Mirabell Gardens (baroque formal gardens, Sound of Music filming location); stroll along the Salzach
  • Evening — Dinner at St. Peter Stiftskeller (one of Europe's oldest restaurants, 9th-century monastery)

Day 8 — Sun Jul 19 · → Vienna

  • Morning — Train from Salzburg to Vienna (~2h 25min)
  • ~12:30 PM — Arrive Vienna; check in; drop bags, freshen up
  • ~1:45 PM — Light lunch
  • ~2:45–3:45 PM — Short Old Town orientation walk: Stephansdom (go inside, skip tower); Graben pedestrian street; Pestsäule plague column; Hofburg area
  • 4:00–5:45 PM — Kunsthistorisches Museum (KHM): grand staircase, Egyptian antiquities, Kunstkammer, Old Masters (especially Bruegel)
  • Evening — Dinner near MuseumsQuartier / Spittelberg

Day 9 — Mon Jul 20 · Vienna

  • 8:30–9:45 AM — Naschmarkt: breakfast at a café stall; browse produce and deli vendors
  • 10:30 AM–12:00 PM — Schönbrunn Palace Grand Tour (~16 state rooms)
  • 12:00–1:00 PM — Formal gardens; casual lunch on site
  • 1:00–2:00 PM — Hedge maze / Labyrinth (teen-friendly)
  • 2:00–3:00 PM — Gloriette climb — panoramic views over the palace and Vienna
  • 3:00–4:00 PM — Downtime; Schönbrunn in July is hot and crowded
  • Late afternoon — Rest at hotel
  • Evening — Dinner + lit-up stroll: Opera House exterior, Albertina terrace, Hofburg / Michaelerplatz
  • Optional: Tiergarten Schönbrunn (world's oldest zoo) if the teens want it

Day 10 — Tue Jul 21 · Vienna

  • 9:00–11:30 AM — Upper Belvedere Palace: Klimt's "The Kiss" and the full Secessionist/Art Nouveau collection; formal gardens
  • 12:00–1:00 PM — Lunch near Belvedere
  • ~2:30–5:30 PM — Riesenrad: Vienna's 1897 giant Ferris wheel (panoramic views); explore Wurstelprater amusement park
  • Evening — Final dinner in the 1st district; last Ringstrasse stroll

Day 11 — Wed Jul 22 · Fly home

  • ~8:00 AM — Checkout; airport train to VIE
  • 10:50 AM — Depart VIE nonstop to Washington Dulles
  • 2:35 PM EDT — Arrive IAD
