| East Crete | 3 nights | Agios Nikolaos / Elounda or Heraklion-area hotel |
| Athens | 1 night | Central Athens or airport-adjacent |

---

## Weather / Crowds

July is peak summer. Expect hot, dry weather, strong sun, and heavier crowds at signature sites.

Practical rule:
- Archaeology before 10 a.m.
- Beaches/pools midday
- Town wandering after 6 p.m.

Crete is generally more comfortable than Athens due to sea breezes and more distributed tourism.
